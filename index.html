About Us

Yasuragi Karate Dojo was founded to empower individuals through authentic karate and practical self-defense training. Inspired by the Japanese word Yasuragi, meaning peace, our dojo teaches discipline, confidence, and inner strength.

We blend traditional karate principles with modern self-defense techniques to help students stay strong, safe, and prepared.

Why Self Defence is Important

At Yasuragi Karate Dojo, we believe self-defense is more than blocking a punch, it is the confidence to protect yourself, stay aware, and stay safe in any situation. Our training teaches you how to recognize danger early, react wisely, and defend yourself effectively without unnecessary violence.

What Self-Defense Gives You

  • Confidence: You walk with calm assurance, not fear.
  • Awareness:You learn to read your environment and avoid danger before it happens
  • Strength & Balance:Your body becomes stronger, faster, and more coordinated.
  • ControlYou gain discipline and emotional stability, not aggression.
  • Protection:You learn practical techniques that anyone can use regardless of size, age, or strength.

Our Approach

Our self-defense training is simple, realistic, and focused on real-life scenarios. We teach how to stay safe at home, on the street, in public spaces, and during unexpected confrontations.

Self-defense is not about fighting
it's about staying safe, staying calm, and staying in control.
